Tools

A collection of frontend-only HTML5 utilities. Everything runs in your browser, nothing is sent to a server. Source on GitHub.

Dev

Base64 Encode / Decode

Encode text to Base64 or decode Base64 back to text. Supports URL-safe alphabet and padding options.

Media

Slideshow

Upload images or PDFs, reorder with drag-and-drop, and present a fullscreen slideshow with optional auto-advance timer.

Board Games

Game Picker

Load your BGG collection CSV and find what to play. Filters by player count (with BGG community recommendations), playtime, weight, and rating.
